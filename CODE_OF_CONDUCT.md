# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and welcoming, especially to beginners.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, trolling, publishing others' private
information, and other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer
**[@faysaliteng](https://github.com/faysaliteng)**. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions
that violate this Code of Conduct, and may ban temporarily or permanently any contributor
for behaviors they deem inappropriate.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
