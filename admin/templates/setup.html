3Auto-forward to your cold wallet (optional)

Automatically move every payment to a cold wallet you control. Needs the dedicated wallet's spend key ({{ '✓ present' if summary.sweep_key_present else 'generate one above first' }}) and a little native coin in the gas tank per chain. Your cold wallet's seed is never stored here.

⛽ Gas tanks — fund these to enable auto-sweep

The same address on every chain. Send a little native coin (~$3–5 worth of BNB / ETH / POL) to each — that's the fuel the gateway uses to forward payments to your cold wallet. Buyers are still credited even with an empty tank; the money just waits until there's gas.
